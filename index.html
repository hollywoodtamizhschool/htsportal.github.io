About the School

Rooted in Tradition,
Thriving in Hollywood

The Hollywood Tamil School is a beloved community institution dedicated to preserving and celebrating Tamil language and culture among Tamil-American families in the greater Los Angeles area.

Founded in 2017 by a small group of passionate parents and educators, our school has grown into a thriving weekend learning community serving students across various regions with the convenience of flexible hybrid schooling — offering both in-person and virtual classes

Inspired by the immortal words of Thiruvalluvar — our school's patron — we strive to give every child a deep, unshakeable connection to their Tamil roots, while helping them flourish as proud Tamil-Americans.

Our volunteers gather every week to provide a warm, structured environment where children learn Tamil script, language, music, classical dance, literature, and festivals.
